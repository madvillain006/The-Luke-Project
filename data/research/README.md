# Research Data Corpus

This directory holds collected research inputs before they are promoted into parsed state, reports, or trading logic.

- `bars/ES/`: ES bar data.
- `bars/SPX/`: SPX bar data.
- `saty/`: Saty exports and source pulls.
- `mancini/`: Mancini source material.
- `bobby/`: Bobby source material.
- `dubz/`: Dubz source material.
- `gex/`: GEX source material.
- `katbot/`: Katbot source material.
- `quarantine/`: Untrusted, malformed, duplicate, or not-yet-classified inputs.
