Trading Pathway: Now To Trusted Desk

Input
Raw sources enter Radar/Katbot/Sybil. Sybil dumps, Discord exports, Kat messages, saved notes, articles, manual paste, and future voice notes enter append-only. Provenance stays attached. No more "where did that idea come from?" detective show at 8:58 AM.
Parse
Signals become structured context. Katbot extracts levels, bias, options/futures context, timestamps, confidence ingredients, and source metadata. Sybil adds narrative/contradiction context. Radar tags themes and review priority.
Compare
The trading-brain workstream compares fresh context to existing Luke memory. Mancini/Saty/Kat/Sybil/Pine levels get reconciled against replay/history, local state, source health, and current thesis after that work lands. Contradictions become review items, not ignored footnotes.
Display
Pine and the Trading Window show the useful truth. Pine becomes the chart-side visual companion. Luke Trading Window becomes the review cockpit. Operator V2 remains evidence/proof, not the front door.
Decide
Luke recommends, refuses, or asks. The decision layer is human-gated. If data is stale, Luke says stale. If confidence is insufficient, Luke says skip/review. If the setup is strong, Luke produces a verifiable plan, not a magic trick with candles.
Prove
Execution stays blocked until proof matures. Live/staged execution remains locked behind explicit proof phases, broker gates, market data proof, TradingView compile checks, and human signoff. This is not fear. This is how grown-up machines avoid setting the curtains on fire.

Current hard boundaries

  • Trading is read-only/replay by default.
  • Research rules remain watchlist/research only until promoted by proof.
  • Pine is visual/watchlist unless separately compiled and approved.
  • Live broker submission requires separate explicit gates and proof.

What "perfected for now" means

  • Current components have been tightened to match the evidence available.
  • User-preferred UI behavior has been preserved and improved where this workstream touched it.
  • Known dirty/old surfaces have been contained or demoted.
  • The next work is targeted integration, not broad wandering.
The trading pathway is intentionally conservative. That is the feature, not the apology.
